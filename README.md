## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing business sales data to identify revenue trends, product performance, category contribution, and regional sales insights.
The objective is to help businesses make better data-driven decisions by understanding their sales performance.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance
* Identify top-performing products and categories
* Study regional sales distribution
* Understand revenue trends over time
* Provide business recommendations based on data insights

---

## Tools & Technologies Used

* Power BI
* Data Analytics
* CSV Dataset

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset includes the following columns:

* Order Date
* Region
* Category
* Sub-Category
* Product Name
* Sales
* Quantity
* Profit

---

## Dashboard Features

The Power BI dashboard includes the following visualizations:

* Sales Trend Analysis (Line Chart)
* Sales by Category (Bar Chart)
* Top 10 Products by Sales
* Regional Sales Distribution (Pie Chart)
* Key Performance Indicators (KPI Cards)


---

## Key Insights

* Technology category generated the highest sales revenue.
* The West region shows the strongest sales performance.
* Some products generate high sales but lower profit margins.
* Monthly sales trends show increased activity during certain periods.

---

## Business Recommendations

* Increase marketing focus on high-performing regions.
* Improve pricing strategy for low-profit products.
* Invest more in top-selling product categories.
* Optimize inventory for high-demand products.
